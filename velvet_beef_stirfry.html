Beef stir fry

0

0


neccesary ingridients

  • beef
  • seasoning
  • kecap manis
  • oil
  • corn starch
  • Baking Soda

1. put baking soda on your beef

while putting the baking soda on the beef ensure that you pour in enough water for the baking soda to dissolve, then proceed with mixing

2. let the meat rest in the fridge

let the meat rest in the fridge for at least an hour

3. wash the meat

after letting the meat rest wash off any excess baking soda

4. season any meat

put any seasoning on your meat

5. put cornstarch on your meat

after seasoning the meat please mixin some cornstarch along the seasoning

6. prepare the pan(preferebly a wok)

prepare the pan by applying the oil to the pan, at least enough to make a small puddle, after that heat up the pan and wait until smoke shows from the pan

7. fry up the beef

fry up the beef, and hear the sizzle

8. apply the kecap manis on the beef, while the beef is frying

while the meat is frying and cooking in the pan, apply the kecap manis onto the beef while also mixing to ensure the beef is properly marinated by the sauce

7. serve up and eat

serve up the beef, in any way you please
